Lethi'Moto Background

Lethi'Moto was founded by three(3) village boys in 2021.It was created as a side hustle for the three gentlemen, to provide for their families. Based in the heart of Limpopo, in a village called Nobody, Lethi'Moto aims to provide the best cleaning services for your car, using on the the best products to make your car clean and shining for weeks. Founded by Phineus Masela(24), Lethabo Mapela(28), Beko Dlamini(20).

Lethi'Moto Carwash Vision

To lead in the carwash industry, known for it's excellent service and excellent results. To be known for it's consistency and commitment to clean cars.

Lethi'Moto Mission

Lethi'Moto Mission is to gain more exposure and more customers to gain more revenue. Make sure our customers feel welcomed and go back home happy and satisfied with the results our services produced. All vehicles are welcome.
